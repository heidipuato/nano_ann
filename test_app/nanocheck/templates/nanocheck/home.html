Nanotoxicity testing is an important step in the creation of nanomaterials to ensure that these materials are safe and not harmful to their intended biological entity, as well as to the environment.

In this application, using machine learning models, we created a nanotoxicity tester for convenient testing of nanoparticles. By combining the concepts of QSAR modeling and Perturbation Theory, we came up with an application that could help predict the toxicological profile of a nanoparticle.
